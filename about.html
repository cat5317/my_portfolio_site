about

My unconventional journey began upon my graduation from Truman State University where I recieved my degree in Agricultural Science. Three years later, the company I was working for declared bankruptcy and closed. I took this as an opportunity to reflect on and re-evaluate my career options. I have aways loved designing and creating. As the ever inqusitive student, I returned to school to obtain a degree in Graphic Communications.

This lead me to my first encounter with web design. I had a great teacher, who helped instill a sense of wonder and excitement about the web; the limitless possibilities, the accessability, the wealth of information. I took the two available web design classes my program had to offer.

Through social media and friends I learned about LaunchCode and the CoderGirl program. I joined a diverse group of women for the Winter 2019 CodeGirl cohort in the Web Development track.
